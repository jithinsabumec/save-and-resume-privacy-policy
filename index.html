Save & Resume Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Save & Resume Chrome extension ("Save & Resume", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the extension.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data to provide the extension's core functionality:

Category Examples Why We Use It Where It Is Stored
Google account profile data User ID (uid), display name, email, profile photo URL Sign-in, account display, and linking your data to your account Local extension storage and Firebase Authentication / Google identity services
Authentication session data ID token, refresh token, token expiry Keep you signed in and refresh your session securely Local extension storage; token refresh handled via Google Secure Token API
Saved timestamp data YouTube video ID, title, timestamp position, thumbnail URL, category name, saved-at time Save and organize timestamps, sync across your devices Chrome local storage and your Firebase Firestore document

2. How Information Is Collected

  • When you sign in using Google through the extension popup.
  • When you save or update timestamps on YouTube pages.
  • When the extension syncs local data with Firebase Firestore.

3. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and maintain signed-in sessions.
  • Store, retrieve, organize, and sync saved timestamps.
  • Migrate legacy local data to cloud storage when you sign in.
  • Operate and secure the extension.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to run the extension:

  • Google Identity / OAuth services for sign-in.
  • Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for authentication and sync storage.

5. Data Retention

  • Local extension data remains until you clear extension storage or uninstall the extension.
  • Cloud timestamp data remains in Firestore until it is deleted through extension actions or support request.
  • Auth session data is cleared locally when you sign out.

6. Data Security

We use authenticated access controls and Firebase security rules intended to restrict each user to their own data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we use commercially reasonable measures to protect data.

7. Your Choices and Controls

  • You may sign out at any time from the extension popup.
  • You may remove saved timestamps and categories inside the extension UI.
  • You may uninstall the extension to remove local extension data.

8. Children's Privacy

Save & Resume is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries where Google and Firebase infrastructure operates.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at this URL and revise the effective date.
